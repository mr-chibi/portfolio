Mr_Chibi's Player's Shops

  • Mr_Chibi's Player Shops is a minecraft datapack!
  • It's Singleplayer & Multiplayer Friendly!
  • Players can create shops using chest GUI and receive block, item and misc from secondary chest that spawns underneath the villager chest.

What Unique Features does pack offer?

  • Player's can access the villager to trade and purchase player goods.
  • If Player, doesn't own shop they can't access chest GUI & Balance.
  • Player's Selling or Buying Items
  • Limited 7 Stacks per Trade!
  • Shops are linked to Player's UUID and cannot be removed from other players.

How it works:

  • Step 1: /trigger ps_create
  • Step 2: Right click chest villager is standing on
  • Step 3: [First Slot, (Buy)] [Second Slot, (Buy 2)] [Third Slot (Sell)]
  • Step 4: Trade villager
  • Step 5: Second chest under villager is where Items / money end up.
  • Step 6: /trigger ps_remove (If you don't want shop anymore it will remove it!)

How it was written in:

  • Json
  • Mcfunction
